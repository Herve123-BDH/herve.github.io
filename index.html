IDEG launches advocacy document on multiparty Local governance today

The institute for Democratic governance(IDEG) will today, november 7, 2018 launch the IDEG advocacy Document to educate and mobilise informed and popular support for the amendment of article 55(3) and the subsequent establishment of a multiparty local governance system in the country

President Nana Addo-Dankwa Akuffo-Addo in this state of nation Message to parliament on february 8, 2018 called for an amendment through a referendment of article 55 of the constitution to enable political parties participate in the election of MMDCEs.

IDEG believes that it is crucial to raise public awareness of the most important decision to reform the local governance architecture of the fourth Republic and to effectively mobilise citizens to support the decision to devolve more power and resources to advance goal
